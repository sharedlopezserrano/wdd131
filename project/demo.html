Welcome to PRESSTART

This site provides information about our restaurant, including Contact, Menu, and About Us pages.

This site is for enjoying a restaurant with thematic games and retro games to share and have fun with your family.

The name "PRESSTART" was chosen to evoke nostalgia for classic gaming, where pressing start begins the fun.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best dish that you have?

Our signature dish is the "Retro Burger" inspired by classic gaming themes.

You can find our contact information on the "Contact Us" page or at the footer of this site.

Absolutely! We offer private bookings for birthdays, corporate events, and more. Contact us for availability.

Yes, we have a variety of vegetarian and vegan dishes available. Check our menu for more details.

Is the restaurant family-friendly?

Definitely! PRESSTART is designed to be a fun and welcoming environment for families of all ages.
